Our Story

PeakFuel was inspired by our founder, Lisa Monroe. Lisa had a difficult time working out because she always felt dehydrated. She wasn't sure why purely water wasn't doing the trick. When doing heavy cardio, she felt light headed, her body would cramp, and she would need to go home. Sometimes she would even feel like fainting. Until she realized that she needed electrolites in her body to keep her fueld and prepared for any workout. She wanted to make this discovery known to anyone who could not fulfill their fitness goals due to dehydration. She wanted to make something with zero sugar that would be tasty and refreshing. She developed PeakFuel to satifsy the cool, refreshing flavor of a workout drink with less sugar.
